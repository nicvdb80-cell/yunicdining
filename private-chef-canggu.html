Canggu

The setting

Canggu and the surrounding area — Berawa, Pererenan, Cemagi, Seseh — have some of the most beautiful private villas in Bali. Large open kitchens, outdoor dining areas, infinity pools with rice field views. These spaces were made for an evening like this.

The experience

What we bring to your villa

We arrive with everything. The ingredients we sourced that morning from local markets. The equipment, the serving items, the staff. Your only responsibility is your guests. We handle the rest — from the first jamu welcome drink to the last dessert.

Questions

Before you book

Do you travel to all areas of Canggu?

Yes. We serve any villa or private residence in Canggu and the surrounding area. Just share your address when you get in touch and we will confirm.

How much notice do you need?

We recommend at least 48 hours. For weekends and peak season, earlier is better. If you are on short notice, contact us anyway — we will do our best.

What do I need to provide?

Just the table and your guests. We bring every ingredient, all cooking equipment, serving items and staff. Nothing is required from you except a space to cook and people to feed.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes. Our Pescatarian and Vegetarian menus are designed for this. Please mention any allergies or specific requirements when you get in touch.

What is the price?

All menus are IDR 1,100,000 per person for groups of 4–14 guests. Beverages and small additions are available separately.
