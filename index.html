01 — Our Story

Yuli carries the memory.
Her team carries the craft.

Yuli grew up making pindang the way her family always made it — with tomat ranti, a small sour tomato her mother added to the broth that you won't find in any recipe book. She still adds it. It's the difference between pindang and her pindang.

Around her stands a culinary team with many years of experience across restaurants, private dining and luxury resorts — the organisation and precision of a professional kitchen, placed in service of flavours that began in a family home. What arrives at your table was never fusion. It is a translation of European style with Indonesian soul.

Family recipes, carried by a team that has cooked them for years.

The Yunic Kitchen
