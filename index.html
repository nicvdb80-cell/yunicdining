Who We Are

Born from family.
Inspired by love.

Yunic Dining began with a simple story. Chef Nic met Yuli, and Yuli began cooking her family recipes for him at home. Her spices, her sambals, and the way she cooked with warmth and instinct — Nic discovered a deeper side of Indonesian food. He often says he literally fell in love through the stomach.

Whenever people asked Nic who inspired him the most in Indonesian cuisine, his answer was always the same: first, his wife Yuli, and then the Indonesian kitchen team he worked with over the years. Even his signature Venison Rendang at Apéritif was built from the base of Yuli's family recipe.

For years, Nic and Yuli invited friends, chefs, and food lovers into their home to taste real Indonesian food cooked with heart. What started as private dinners slowly grew into something too special not to share. Yunic Dining was created to bring that same feeling to others.

Yuli now guides guests through each menu — explaining the dishes, the spices, the family memories, and the little secrets behind the recipes. Around 90% of ingredients are locally sourced, with a zero-waste kitchen philosophy: trimmings become sambals, ferments, and flavor bases. Homemade fermented spice pastes and shio koji add depth while respecting the original flavors.

— Yuli & Nic
What We Offer

Private Indonesian dining,
brought to your home.

You simply sit back, relax, and enjoy.

Yunic Dining creates intimate VIP culinary journeys for families, friends, villa dinners, celebrations, and special gatherings. We bring the warmth of Indonesian hospitality directly to your home — so you do not need to travel through traffic, organize a restaurant booking, or worry about the details.

Our experience is more than a dinner. It is a guided journey through Indonesia's islands, flavors, spices, and family stories. Each menu is inspired by authentic home cooking, Yuli's family recipes, and the rich culinary traditions she grew up with. From slow-cooked rendang to vibrant sambals, fragrant broths, coastal seafood, rice, vegetables, and sharing dishes — every course is created to feel generous, soulful, and deeply connected to place.

We take care of the food, the service, the storytelling, and the flow of the evening. Your guests will be served with care, introduced to the dishes, and invited to discover the real taste of Indonesian cuisine in a relaxed and elegant setting.

Yunic Dining is for those who want more than food on a table. It is for those who want to create memories, share stories, and experience Indonesia from the comfort of home.
