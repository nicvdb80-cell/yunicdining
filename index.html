Our Story

Born from family.
Rooted in the archipelago.

Yunic Dining is a celebration of Indonesian food, family heritage, and the stories behind every dish. It is inspired by the authentic home cooking of Yuli's family and the culinary journey that taught us the depth, richness, and diversity of Indonesia's flavors.

From Sumatra's slow-cooked rendang to the coastal freshness of Maluku and North Sulawesi, every menu is designed as a journey — warm, generous, fragrant, and deeply connected to place.

Yunic brings this experience into your home: intimate, elegant, and full of stories, with food made to be shared.

— Yuli

The Menus

Choose your journey

Three curated voyages through the Indonesian archipelago. Each menu is a complete culinary story — from first bite to sweet farewell.

Signature

The Authentic Yunic Journey

West Sumatra · North Sulawesi · Java · Bali
Deep Rich Ceremonial Aromatic

Our signature Indonesian journey. A generous and soulful menu inspired by family recipes, regional traditions, and the rich flavors of the archipelago. Expect dishes such as Rendang Sapi from West Sumatra, Ikan Woku from North Sulawesi, warm broths, sambals, snacks, and desserts served in a refined sharing style.

Best for Guests who want the full Yunic experience — the complete culinary voyage across the islands.
Pescatarian

The Yunic Ocean Journey

Maluku · Sulawesi · Bali · Sumatra
Fresh Citrusy Coastal Spicy

A pescatarian journey inspired by Indonesia's islands, coastlines, fishing villages, and spice routes. This menu highlights fish, seafood, fresh herbs, citrus, coconut, sambal, and coastal flavors from regions such as Maluku, Sulawesi, Bali, and Sumatra.

Best for Guests who love seafood, freshness, and aromatic coastal Indonesian flavors.
Vegetarian

The Yunic Garden Journey

Java · Bali · Yogyakarta · Village Kitchens
Earthy Nutty Herbal Soulful

A vegetarian journey through Indonesia's gardens, rice fields, markets, and village kitchens. This menu celebrates vegetables, tempeh, tofu, coconut, peanuts, jackfruit, herbs, sambals, and slow-cooked bumbu with the same depth and generosity as the signature journey.

Best for Guests who want a plant-based Indonesian experience without losing richness, spice, or soul.
