FAQ

Common questions

How do I book? +

Contact us via WhatsApp at +62 819 9480 7407 or email yunicdining@gmail.com. We'll confirm availability and details within a few hours.

What is the price per person? +

All three menus are IDR 1,100,000 per person. Beverages and à la carte additions are priced separately.

What is the minimum group size? +

We cook for groups of 4 to 14 guests. Smaller or larger groups — contact us and we'll discuss what's possible.

Where in Bali do you serve? +

Ubud, Canggu, Seminyak, Uluwatu, Nusa Dua and most villa locations across Bali. Just tell us your address.

Do I need to provide anything? +

Just your table and your guests. We bring all ingredients, cooking equipment, serving items and staff.

How long does the evening take? +

Around 2.5 to 3 hours from arrival to the final dessert. We work at the pace of your group.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements? +

Yes. Our Pescatarian and Vegetarian menus are designed with this in mind. Please mention any allergies or requirements when booking.

Do you serve alcohol? +

We offer a curated selection of wine, aperitif cocktails and soft drinks. We can also work with your existing villa bar.

Can I customise the menu? +

Each menu is fixed by design — this allows us to cook with focus and quality. However, we can discuss small adjustments for dietary needs.

What happens if I need to cancel? +

Contact us as soon as possible. We'll do our best to reschedule. Cancellation policy is discussed at the time of booking.
