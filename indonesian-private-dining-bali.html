The philosophy

Food as hospitality, not performance.

Indonesian family cooking is generous, layered and deeply personal. Yunic Dining does not replicate restaurant service — we recreate the feeling of being welcomed into an Indonesian home. Stories come with every dish.

The ingredients

From the market to your table.

We source daily from local Balinese markets. Fresh spices, vegetables, coconut, lime and the regional ingredients that make Indonesian cooking irreplaceable. Nothing is prepared days in advance — everything is cooked fresh for your evening.

The experience

An evening that stays with you.

FAQ

Common questions

What is Indonesian private dining? +

It is a dining experience where an Indonesian chef and host come to your home or villa and cook and serve an authentic Indonesian menu for your group privately.

How is Yunic different from a private chef? +

Yunic is not a catering service. We are storytellers as much as cooks. Yuli brings the cultural memory of Indonesian family cooking; Chef Nic brings the craft. Every dish comes with its origins.

What languages do you speak? +

English and Indonesian. Our service is delivered warmly in both.
