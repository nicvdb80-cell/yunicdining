How it works

We come to your villa, fully prepared.

We arrive as a small team, set up quietly and create the full experience around your table. Cooking, serving, storytelling and hosting. Each menu is fixed and prepared for the whole group, allowing us to focus entirely on the quality of the meal and the warmth of the evening.

The menus

Three Indonesian journeys. Choose one for your table.

Choose from the Authentic Yunic Journey (meat, seafood, rice and sambal), the Coastal Journey (fish, shellfish and coconut broth) or the Garden Journey (tempeh, fermentation and vegetables). All menus are IDR 1,100,000 per person for groups of 4–14 guests. View all menus →

Areas we serve

Anywhere in Bali.

We serve private dining across Bali — Ubud, Canggu, Seminyak, Uluwatu, Nusa Dua and beyond. If you're unsure whether we travel to your location, just ask via WhatsApp.

FAQ

Common questions

What do I need to provide? +

Just the table and your guests. We bring all ingredients, equipment, serving items and staff.

How much advance notice do you need? +

We recommend booking at least 48 hours ahead. For weekends and high season, earlier is better.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements? +

Yes. Our Pescatarian and Vegetarian menus are designed with this in mind. Please let us know any specific requirements when booking.

Is this suitable for special occasions? +

Absolutely. Many guests choose Yunic Dining for birthdays, anniversaries, honeymoons and family gatherings.
